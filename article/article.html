Why Trees Are Great

David Sibiriakov, 20:52 Tuesday, 7 January 2025

I love trees so much! They absorb CO2 from out atmosphere and release oxygen for us to breathe.

They look great. Green is a good color! That's another reason to love them. In the colder months of the Northern hemisphere winter, they do turn brown-grey. However, pines and other trees of the family look wonderful all-year-round.

Forests make perfect hikes. Depending on the vegetation density they could be more enjoyable when passing is a breeze. If it's a mountainy area, you get a solid chance of observing breathtaking ranges in the distance.

British landscapes are a vibe, but their outlook is somewhat bland because how treeless it often is. This is due to years of deforestation and animal farming. Sheep would eat everything, including little tree saplings.

My favourite kind of tree is redwood found in California. They have wallpapers with them on the newest MacOS 15, Sequoia! A quick google search tells that the scientific name those trees come by is Sequoioideae, very appropriate. I would love to see the national park in-person sometime.
